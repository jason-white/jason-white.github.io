Rebel Kale

Rebel Kale was a project for a graphic design course where students were asked to create a company and create all of the design work. Tasks included multiple logos, a poster, website or mobile application and a brochure.

The design choices made for Rebel Kale are to give an edge to a food truck that only serves vegan and vegetarian foods. The goal is to attract people of all dieting practices strategically with design, humor and a well-rounded menu.

Role: Concept, Design, Photography, Print
