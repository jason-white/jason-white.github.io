Trust Your Barber

This was a fun, multi-process piece that was created while taking a lithography class. The text in French translates to "Have confidence in your barber."

The original image was taken from a photojournalism piece on a local barber shop in Greensboro, North Carolina. A vector of the image was created, printed and then transfered to a lithograph stone via acetone.

Role: Photography, Design, Acetone transfer, Print
